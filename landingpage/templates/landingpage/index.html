The What

We’re a couple of UVA undergrad students building the first-ever marketplace of opinions. Using our unique market-based platform (think of something like the stock market, but not for shares of companies), you and other UVA students use tokens and demand-supply economics to rank anything. But we want to start small, and we want to prove it works while adding value to the UVA community.


So the very first opinion asset ranked on Unibursal will be something you’re all too familiar with - UVA courses.

When you think a UVA course should be ranked higher, you buy tokens of that course - sort of like a weighted upvote, depending on how many you buy - and then selling them when you think they’re at their peak (a weighted downvote). As you and your peers do this, the course’s ranking will arrive at a level that more accurately reflects the opinions of everyone. And as opinions change constantly depending on the latest information, so too will the ranking - it’s all reflected in the market. And yes, we did say you need to spend money to vote on Unibursal. You might have some reservations, understandably, but as you read on, we hope you’ll see why. Be sure to check out our FAQ to learn more, or reach out to us with another question!


The Why

To Quantify Opinion

You know what your own opinion is on the classes you’ve taken, and maybe you know the opinion of a few friends and strangers on Reddit, but it’s pretty much impossible to gauge what all your peers think -

about any class, at any given moment (like after a professor drops a surprise quiz??). And simple Like and Upvote/Downvote systems don’t tell the whole story. Where’s the distinction between “it wasn’t bad” and “I liked it” and “I loved it”? Enter Unibursal. We’re going somewhere nobody has gone before, connecting people’s opinions in a comprehensive, dynamic way that just makes sense.

To Share Information

Listen, we love theCourseForum and RateMyProfessors but part of the reason we built this platform is because they, and other review-based systems don’t tell the full story -

none of the people we’ve asked have ever left a review on them despite enjoying or disliking a number of classes and professors. So at the very least, we hope the unique structure and benefits of Unibursal will make us a tool for sharing information that other review-based websites have missed.

To Empower Decision-Making

You make a lot of choices based on simple facts, but you also make a lot of choices based on other people’s opinions -

what shows to binge, where to get dinner, what party to go to, and oftentimes, what class to take. We hope Unibursal helps you make those decisions based on an accurate view of the community’s opinion. And if you do have an opinion to offer, you have a chance to profit!

And More

We’ve set our sights on gauging all sorts of ideas and opinions soon, but right now our focus is on answering these course-related questions:

1) What do you think about this class?
2) What does the rest of UVA think about it?
3) Can you make any decision or take any action based on that information?

If you've got an answer to those questions - or want one - we want you to join Unibursal

The How



01

Buy a token of a class that you have a positive opinion of and that you think others do too. As demand for a token rises, so does its price. What value do I get from holding a token?

02

Sell the token when you feel that the class is accurately ranked compared to other classes on the market - or simply, when you don’t think its price is going to go up any further. How do I know I’ll be able to actually sell a token once I’ve bought it?

03

When enough people participate in voting their opinions (buying and selling tokens), our unique interpretation of a Nobel-awarded theory says that the prices of these tokens will reflect where the community's opinion stands. So, the class with the highest-priced token is held the highest in community opinion.
